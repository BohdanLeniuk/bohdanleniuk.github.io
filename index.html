Bohdan Leniuk

I am a fifth year Ph.D. candidate in economics at the University of Georgia and will be on the academic job market during the 2026-2027 cycle.

I study both macroeconomics and labor economics. My job market paper investigates the long-term consequences of receiving a promotion and uses this setting to explore the effect of policy on sorting in the labor market.
